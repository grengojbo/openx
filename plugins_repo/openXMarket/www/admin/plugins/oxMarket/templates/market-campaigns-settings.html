Earn more revenue from your existing campaigns with OpenX Market

OpenX Market is a free service that places higher paying ads on your site. Use it to make more money without taking on more risk.
To participate, select the campaigns you want OpenX Market to compete with and assign a floor price (minimum CPM) to each campaign. You do not need to retag your zones.

{/if}

The minimum CPM you provide for a specific campaign will be the floor price OpenX Market uses for that campaign. If the highest bid from OpenX Market exceeds the campaign's floor price, OpenX Market will serve the ad and pay you the higher revenue. Otherwise, your campaign's existing ad will be delivered.

We recommend that you specify a CPM that represents the effective CPM that you expect to get from your campaign.

Opting in your campaigns to OpenX Market

OpenX Market competes with your campaigns to provide you with higher paying ads. Select which campaigns to opt in and set a floor price for each one. OpenX Market must pay higher than the floor or the campaign's original ad is served.

Tip: To maximize revenue uplift, set a campaign's floor price close to the CPM it is already earning.
