Maximize your revenue by flowing inventory from your campaigns into OpenX Market.

OpenX Market is a free ad exchange that places higher paying ads on your site. Choose which campaigns to opt in and assign minimum CPMs to your inventory. OpenX Market will serve ads if it beats the campaign's floor price, otherwise your original ad is served.

You do not need to retag your zones to participate. To learn more about OpenX Market ads and getting paid, visit the FAQ.
